## What Is This Platform?

Constructive Solutions Ibiza is a construction job platform.

It connects:
- **Clients** who need work done
- **Tradespeople** who can do the work

The platform helps them find each other, communicate, agree a price, complete the job, and leave a review.

Everything in the system is built around one core object: **A Job**.

Examples: Microcement floor, Bathroom renovation, Electrical repair, Kitchen installation.

Every job becomes a project file inside the platform containing: messages, quotes, photos, progress, reviews.

---

## How Clients Use the Platform

### Step 1 — Post a Job
The client uses the **Job Wizard** which asks structured questions:
- What trade do you need?
- What type of work?
- Where is the property?
- What budget?
- Upload photos

This creates a clear job description.

### Step 2 — The Platform Finds Tradespeople
The system matches the job to tradespeople who offer that specific service.

Example: Job = "Microcement floor" → only microcement installers see it. Not electricians, not plumbers.

### Step 3 — Tradespeople Respond
Tradespeople can message the client, ask questions, and send a quote — all inside the job file.

### Step 4 — Client Accepts a Quote
The client chooses the best quote. Once accepted: Job status → `in_progress`.

### Step 5 — Job Completed
When work is finished: client marks complete, leaves a review. That review builds the tradesperson's reputation.

---

## How Tradespeople Use the Platform

### Step 1 — Create a Professional Profile
Choose services offered (e.g. Carpentry, Electrical, Microcement, Bathroom installation).

### Step 2 — Receive Matching Jobs
The platform shows jobs matching their skills (e.g. "Microcement floor in Ibiza Town, Budget €4,000–€6,000").

### Step 3 — Send a Quote
Structured quotes: Labour €2,500 + Materials €1,200 + Transport €200 = Total €3,900.

### Step 4 — Complete the Job
Communicate with client → complete work → receive review. Good work = more jobs.

---

## The 5 Main Systems

| System | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| **Job Wizard** | Structured job creation (prevents vague "I need a builder" requests) |
| **Matching** | Connects jobs to specialists (microcement jobs → microcement installers) |
| **Messaging** | In-platform communication, always linked to a job |
| **Quotes** | Structured proposals (labour, materials, costs) for easy comparison |
| **Reviews** | Post-job ratings that build tradesperson reputation and trust |

---

## Why This Platform Is Different

Most construction platforms: Post job → send leads → conversation moves to WhatsApp.

Constructive Solutions Ibiza keeps everything organised:

```
Job → Messages → Quotes → Work → Review
```

It's a **construction workflow system**, not just a lead generator.
